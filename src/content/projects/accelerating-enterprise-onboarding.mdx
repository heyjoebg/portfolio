<div class="single-col">
  <h2 class="case-study-subtitle">The Problem</h2>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">Enterprise clients were taking <strong>2–3 weeks to go live</strong>, delaying value and increasing support load. After several onboarding cycles, three key issues surfaced:</p>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">Unclear structure during calls: e-commerce assistants had no standard action plan or script.</p>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">Scattered information: client details were spread across emails, Notion pages, and chat threads.</p>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">No visibility: leadership couldn't see onboarding status or where clients were getting stuck. Without visibility, each onboarding felt like reinventing the wheel. Clients had questions, CSMs had no consistent reference, and leadership had no data to improve.</p>
</div>

<Highlight text="why is onboarding taking weeks?" />

<div class="single-col">
  <h2 class="case-study-subtitle">The Goal</h2>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">Turn a 3-week process into a <strong>repeatable 5-day framework</strong> that ensures: a clear, guided experience for clients, predictable progress tracking for the team, and visibility into onboarding status and time-to-value.</p>

  <h2 class="case-study-subtitle">The Approach</h2>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">We tackled the challenge in <strong>three key fronts</strong>: Process, Tooling, and Visibility.</p>

  <h2 class="case-study-subtitle">1. Creating a Scalable Action Plan</h2>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">I started by mapping every step of onboarding, from kickoff to go-live. With input from CSMs, Sales, and PMs, we built a <strong>day-by-day plan</strong> that set expectations and ownership across teams.</p>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">We introduced a <strong>Pre-Onboarding Intake Form</strong>, sent before kickoff, to collect critical data early:</p>
  <ul role="list" class="list">
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Marketplace connections</li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Single source of truth</li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Inventory size &amp; structure</li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Listing templates</li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Technical contact</li>
  </ul>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">This form ensured that <strong>by Day 1</strong>, both the client and CSM team were aligned and ready.</p>
</div>

<Highlight text="The Pre-Onboarding phase became our secret weapon — by the time we met the client, 70% of setup was already done." />

<div class="single-col">
  <h2 class="case-study-subtitle">2. Building a Fast Prototype with AI</h2>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">We needed something fast, not perfect.</p>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">Using <strong>Figma's AI features (Figma Make)</strong>, I built a "quick and dirty" onboarding tool that:</p>
  <ul role="list" class="list">
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Let clients <strong>submit intake information</strong> before calls.</li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Automatically <strong>triggered an email</strong> to the assigned e-commerce assistant.</li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Reduced manual back-and-forth, saving hours before kickoff.</li>
  </ul>
  <a href="https://slip-crowd-10957170.figma.site/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="hj-button">View Prototype</a>
</div>

<div class="single-col">
  <h2 class="case-study-subtitle">3. Adding Visibility with an Admin Dashboard</h2>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">Once onboarding became structured, we needed a way to track it.</p>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">I designed an <strong>Admin Onboarding Dashboard</strong> to monitor:</p>
  <ul role="list" class="list">
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Average onboarding time per client</li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Steps where users got stuck</li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item">Days spent on each phase</li>
  </ul>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">The dashboard turned anecdotal delays into measurable insights. We could now identify blockers, assign owners, and continuously improve.</p>

<div class="single-col no-margin-bottom">
  <h2 class="case-study-subtitle">The Outcome</h2>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">After the rollout:</p>
  <ul role="list" class="list">
    <li class="case-study-list-item"><strong>Onboarding time dropped from 15+ days to under 5 days</strong></li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item"><strong>Every step was documented</strong>, creating accountability</li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item"><strong>Leadership gained full visibility</strong> into onboarding progress</li>
    <li class="case-study-list-item"><strong>Clients were impressed</strong> by how prepared and organized our team was</li>
  </ul>

  <h2 class="case-study-subtitle">Takeaway</h2>
  <p class="heyjoe_paragraph">Enterprise onboarding doesn't have to be slow. With the right structure, automation, and visibility, we turned a chaotic process into a <strong>predictable, 5-day journey</strong> that scales.</p>
</div>
